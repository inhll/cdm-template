Providing students a general statement about the nature of the module and its relation to the course as a whole helps students:

  • Build a cognitive framework to organize and interpret information in the module.
  • Access their prior knowledge and build connections with what they are about to learn.
  • Understand why the content is relevant to the course as a whole.
