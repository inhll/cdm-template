Module Title

Key Terms and Concepts

Section Context

An overview of key terms and concepts helps students to access the rest of the material more easily. Even if there are only two or three terms, this list can be very helpful for students.

Hint: One way to easily develop a good term and concept list is to give students points (or extra-credit points) for submitting 2 key terms and 1 concept to the Dropbox for each module. Now that you have a starting list, cull it for the most important terms, and revise / expand the definitions for future courses.
