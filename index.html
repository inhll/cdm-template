Removing the Templates

The template was a huge mistake but it's only in the master course. You can come back to the master with fresh eyes later, or remove everything from the master and start again from scratch.

  1. Access your course.
  2. Click CONTENT.
  3. To remove all content including anything you have uploaded into the course to this point, check the box at the top of content (1).
  4. Click the trash can (2).
  5. Select "Delete items from content and delete all associated files from the course files."
  6. Click DELETE

That's all, the template has been completely removed from your course.
